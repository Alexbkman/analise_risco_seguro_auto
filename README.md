# Car Insurance Claim Analysis

## Objective
Analyze claim rates across customer segments and identify key risk drivers to support pricing, underwriting, and retention strategies.

---

## Project Structure

- **EDA Notebook**: Data exploration, cleaning, and insights
- **Power BI Dashboard**: Interactive visualization with key metrics and risk drivers
- **Dataset**: Car insurance claim data

---

## Key Insights

- Young drivers (16–25) with low driving experience (0–9 years) have the highest claim rates
- Low credit score is strongly associated with higher risk
- Risk significantly decreases with age and driving experience

---

## Dashboard Overview

The dashboard includes:

- Overview with KPIs (Total Customers, Total Claims, Claim Rate)
- Risk Drivers analysis (Heatmap, Credit Score, Past Accidents)
- Actionable business recommendations

---

## Recommendations

- Apply risk-based pricing for high-risk segments
- Implement stricter underwriting rules
- Encourage safer driving behavior with incentives
- Segment customers based on risk profile
- Monitor key risk indicators continuously

---

## How to Use

1. Open the Power BI file (.pbix) locally
2. Explore the interactive dashboard
3. Review the EDA notebook for detailed analysis
